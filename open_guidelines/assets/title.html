COIN Training Manual

This manual contains guidelines on basic clinical management of neonates and young infants in health facilities in the Malawian primary and secondary health care level. The content is intended to target medical assistants, clinical officers, nurses and essentially all nursing and clinical staff involved in looking after neonates and young infants.
